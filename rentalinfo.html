What do you need to rent a car?

To rent a car from campervanrental, you will need the following:

    Renting a Car:

  • Be 25 years old (if you’re under 25, you may be required to pay a young driver’s fee, which varies by rental location)

  • A valid driver's license with a photo

  • A photo ID (For example: a driver’s license or passport)

  • Credit or debit card (check the acceptable forms of payment at the location you’re renting from)

    Rental Rates and Fees:

  • Car rental rates can vary based on factors like the location, rental duration, vehicle type, and additional services requested.

  • Rates are typically quoted on a daily basis, and discounts are often available for longer rental periods (e.g., weekly or monthly rentals).

  • Additional fees may apply for extras such as GPS navigation systems, child seats, additional drivers, or fuel service options. Make sure to clarify these fees upfront.

    Insurance and Liability:

  • Rental companies usually offer insurance options, including collision damage waiver (CDW), which covers damage to the rental vehicle, and supplemental liability insurance (SLI), which covers damages to third parties.

  • Check your existing auto insurance policy and credit card benefits, as they may provide coverage for rental cars. If not, consider purchasing insurance from the rental company.

    Rental Terms and Conditions:

  • Carefully read and understand the rental agreement before signing it. Pay attention to details like mileage restrictions, fuel policy, return conditions, and penalties for late returns or damage.

  • Inspect the car thoroughly before driving off the rental lot. Note any existing damages and report them to the rental company to avoid being held responsible for them upon return.

    Returning the Car:

  • Return the rental car on the agreed-upon date and at the designated location.

  • Ensure the car is clean and in the same condition as when you received it.

  • Keep copies of all paperwork, receipts, and inspection reports for your records.
